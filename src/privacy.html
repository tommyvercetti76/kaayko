Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Kaayko ("we," "our," or "us") operates kaayko.com (the "Services"), which include:

  • Paddling Out: AI-powered paddle condition forecasts and water location discovery
  • Kaayko Store: E-commerce platform for apparel and merchandise
  • Karma: Alumni platform for schools

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information. By using our Services, you consent to the data practices described here.

Key Point: We are committed to transparency. You have control over your data and can opt out of data retention at any time.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Purchase Information: Email address, phone number (optional), and shipping address through Stripe's secure payment processing.
  • Order Fulfillment Data: If you consent, we retain shipping info for future order convenience.
  • Location Queries: When using Paddling Out, you may provide location coordinates or lake names.
  • Paddle Ratings: Anonymous condition ratings submitted through the Paddle Trainer. We store a device fingerprint to prevent duplicates, not to identify you.
  • Communications: If you contact us, we may retain your email address and message content.

Automatically Collected

  • Usage Data: Browser type, device info, pages visited, time spent, referring URLs.
  • Cookies: Session management, preferences (dark mode), and Firebase Analytics.
  • IP Addresses: Collected for fraud prevention and rate limiting. Not linked to personal identity.

Payment Information

We do not store credit card information. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. Card details are transmitted directly to Stripe under their privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • Order Fulfillment: Processing purchases, shipping, and order tracking.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage to enhance user experience and ML model accuracy.
  • Paddle Score Calibration: Anonymous ratings are used to improve forecast accuracy per location.
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries.
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent transactions.

Data Retention

Order Data

  • With Consent: If you opt in, we store shipping info in Firebase Firestore for up to 2 years or until you request deletion.
  • Without Consent: Order data is processed through Stripe and not permanently retained by Kaayko.

Paddle and Weather Data

  • Location Queries: Processed in real-time and cached temporarily (up to 3.5 hours). Not linked to personal identifiers.
  • User Ratings: Stored indefinitely as anonymous training data. No account or personal info is attached.

Analytics

  • Firebase Analytics: Usage patterns stored for up to 14 months.
  • Server Logs: API request logs retained for 30 days.

Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with third parties only as needed to operate:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Google Cloud Platform (Firebase): Hosting, database, analytics
  • WeatherAPI.com: Real-time weather data for Paddling Out

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@kaayko.com.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser storage as follows:

  • Local Storage: Remembering preferences like dark mode. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Firebase Auth Tokens: Authentication state is managed by Firebase Auth using IndexedDB, not traditional cookies.
  • Cookies: Used in limited contexts for link attribution and session continuity within Kortex (smart links). Paddling Out does not set cookies.

You can clear browser storage through your browser settings. Clearing local storage will reset your preferences.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Firebase security rules restricting database access
  • Stripe PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • Rate limiting and fraud detection

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware we have, we will delete it promptly.

California (CCPA) & EU (GDPR)

California residents and EU individuals have additional rights under CCPA and GDPR respectively, including the right to know, delete, and opt out. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@kaayko.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
