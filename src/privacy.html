Privacy Policy - Kaayko Paddle Bot

Last updated: October 27, 2025

1. Overview

Kaayko Paddle Bot is a ChatGPT custom GPT that provides real-time paddle condition forecasts using machine learning predictions. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

When you use the Kaayko Paddle Bot, we collect:

  • Location coordinates: Latitude and longitude you provide or that are looked up from lake names
  • Query information: Your questions about paddle conditions

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data solely to:

  • Generate paddle condition forecasts for your requested locations
  • Provide weather and safety information
  • Improve the accuracy of our predictions

4. Data Storage

We do NOT store:

  • Personal identifying information
  • User profiles or accounts
  • Conversation history beyond what ChatGPT retains

Location queries are processed in real-time through our API and are not permanently retained by Kaayko.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service uses:

  • OpenAI ChatGPT: Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy
  • WeatherAPI: For real-time weather data
  • Google Cloud Platform: For hosting our API and ML model

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties except as required to provide the service (weather APIs, cloud hosting).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Request information about data processing
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes.
