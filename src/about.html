      Three things worth your time.

      Paddling Out tells you whether the water is worth it before you drag the kayak out of the garage. ML conditions — wind, gusts, cloud cover — for any lake on the planet. Because "looks fine" from the parking lot has ruined too many mornings. Curated spots with notes from real paddlers, not bots.

      The Store is invite-only for a reason. The art here isn't filler — it's Chanakya, Nagpur, hypothermia jokes, and history most people have forgotten. Printed on things you'll actually wear. If you know, you know.

      Karma connects school alumni with the communities that shaped them. A platform for the networks that matter but never had the right tools. Built for schools that want to stay connected without building software themselves.
